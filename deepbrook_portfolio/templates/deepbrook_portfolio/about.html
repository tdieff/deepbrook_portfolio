Deepbrook Portfolio is a tool to simulate buying and selling stocks.

No real money is used, but stock prices shown in the app reflect actual prices, accurate to 15 minutes. Data is provided by IEX Cloud.

Users can register for an account my creating a username and password. Once signed in, users start with $10,000 in play money. Get price quotes, buy, sell, view your trade history, and watch what happens to the value of your portfolio as the markets move in real life.
